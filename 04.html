Christmas Card Design

In this design I tried to create an unusual composition of figure and landscape which would invoke a festive theme in an unexpected and playfully self-referential manner. I used isolated areas of rich colour against a cold background to create a bold design which would reproduce well on a small scale, and I overlaid textures created with paint splatters and with a nibbed pen to suggest wind and snow.
