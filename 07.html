Sherlock Holmes on the Radio

I developed this as an illustrated packaging design for the BBCâ€™s Sherlock Holmes radio dramatisations, which are included on a USB stick. I wanted to create an object which would be exciting and dramatic to interact with and I devised the front cover image of a door to invoke mystery and intrigue. The bold image of Sherlock Holmes and the lengthy gatefold inside are intended to surprise, and I incorporated several hand-drawn typographical elements into the design to give it a cultured, old-fashioned touch to balance the playfulness of the other elements.
