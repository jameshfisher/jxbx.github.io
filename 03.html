Pickpocket

I created this poster design as a response to Robert Bresson’s film Pickpocket (1959). I was influenced by the simple graphic forms of Polish and Czech film posters of the 1960s and 70s, and I wanted to acknowledge Bresson’s own distinctive minimalist style of framing and composition.

I chose to avoid clearly representing any of the film’s actors in the image, alluding both to Pickpocket’s dominant themes of secrecy and anonymity, and to Bresson’s own tendency to use non-established performers in his films.
